The Problem

🔒
Centralized platforms own your data.
Todoist, Google Tasks, Notion — your tasks live on their servers. They can change terms, shut down, or lock you out at any time.
💸
On-chain storage is expensive.
Storing a 500-character string on Ethereum costs ~126,000 gas. At scale, this makes decentralized apps impractical for everyday use.
⚠
Web3 UX is hard.
Users shouldn't need to understand gas, RPCs, or event syncing just to manage a todo list. The blockchain should be invisible.

The Solution

⛓
Upgradeable smart contract (UUPS proxy)
TodoListV2 with role-based access, circuit breaker, reentrancy guard, and soft-delete — deployed to Sepolia testnet.
📦
IPFS for descriptions — 79% gas savings
Descriptions are pinned to Pinata/IPFS. Only the 46-byte CID is stored on-chain instead of the full 500-char string.
🎯
Seamless Web2-like experience
Real-time event sync caches on-chain data in MongoDB. Users see instant reads with blockchain writes happening in the background.
🔑
Wallet-native auth (EIP-191)
No passwords, no emails. Sign a message with MetaMask, get a JWT. Your address is your identity.
